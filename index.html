About Us

Our name are Diego, Briega, and Zidane. We are students of ABBS Junior High School, Surakarta.

We are a group of students who have undertaken a learning journey in ICT from 7th to 9th grade. During this journey, we have learned various interesting and challenging aspects of information and communication technology.

This final project is the result of our hard work in developing various projects, ranging from hardware construction, creating interactive games, exploring the Internet of Things, to building responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
